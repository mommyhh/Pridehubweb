Server Rules & Guidelines 🏳️‍🌈

By joining Pride Hub, you agree to follow these guidelines at all times.

1. Respect Everyone

Treat all members with kindness and respect. Harassment, bullying, personal attacks, discrimination, or targeting others is not allowed. Respect everyone's identity, pronouns, and chosen name.

2. No Hate Speech

Hate speech, slurs, or discrimination based on sexual orientation, gender identity, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, disability, or age will not be tolerated.

3. Keep Content Appropriate

No NSFW content, pornography, gore, excessive violence, or illegal content. Follow Discord's Terms of Service at all times.

4. No Harassment

Do not threaten, stalk, repeatedly DM, or encourage others to harass members. Respect people's boundaries.

5. Respect Privacy

Never share someone's personal information or private conversations without their permission. Doxxing results in an immediate ban.

6. No Spam

Avoid message spam, emoji spam, GIF spam, ping spam, excessive caps, or repeated content.

7. No Advertising

Advertising Discord servers, social media, businesses, or other communities is only allowed in approved channels with staff permission.

8. Use the Correct Channels

Keep discussions in their appropriate channels to help keep the server organized.

9. Keep Drama Out

Do not bring personal conflicts or outside drama into the server. Contact staff if you need assistance resolving an issue.

10. No Impersonation

Do not pretend to be another member, a staff member, Discord staff, or anyone else.

11. Follow Discord's Rules

All members must follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

12. No Scams

Scams, phishing links, fake giveaways, malicious files, or attempts to deceive members are strictly prohibited.

13. Appropriate Profiles

Usernames, nicknames, profile pictures, and statuses must not contain offensive, hateful, or explicit content.

14. Respect Staff

Listen to staff instructions. If you disagree with a moderation decision, discuss it respectfully through the proper channels.

15. No Mini-Modding

Do not act like a moderator. Report rule-breaking to staff instead of trying to enforce the rules yourself.

16. Voice Chat Rules

No microphone abuse, screaming, disruptive noises, or intentionally ruining conversations for others.

17. No Trolling

Do not intentionally provoke, bait, or disrupt the community for reactions or entertainment.

18. Help Keep Pride Hub Safe

Report rule violations, scams, or unsafe behavior to the staff team. Together we can keep Pride Hub welcoming, inclusive, and enjoyable for everyone.
